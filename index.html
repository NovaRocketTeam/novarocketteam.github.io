About Us

This project was born in Faculdade de Ciências e Tecnologia of NOVA University of Lisbon last year with the will to learn new concepts, explore the unknown and test our limits. The project's team dedicates not just to learning how to design, assemble, and launch rockets, but also to improve its efficiency from paper electronics for weight reduction to rocket motor designs.

Even tought model rocketry is a very popular hobby in other countries in Portugal there is a very small culture. So we also aim to educate and inspire the next generation of engineers.
